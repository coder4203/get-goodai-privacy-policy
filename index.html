1. Interpretations and Definitions

Interpretation Words with capitalized initials have specific meanings defined below. The same definitions apply whether they appear in singular or plural form.

Definitions

Application (App): Refers to GetGood.
Company (We, Us, Our): Refers to GetGood by Yusuf Rana.
Account: A unique user profile created to access the App.
Personal Data: Any identifiable information.
Usage Data: Automatically collected analytics and performance information.
Device: Any device capable of accessing the App.
Service Providers: Third-party companies supporting the App.
You: The individual using the App.

2. Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

2.1 Types of Data Collected

Personal Data We may collect the following information:

  • Account Information – Email & authentication data.
  • Profile Details – Display name, age range, goals (optional).
  • Fitness Data – Manually logged workouts & progress.
  • Communication Data – Messages or support requests.

Usage Data includes:

  • Device type & OS
  • Interaction metrics
  • Crash logs & diagnostics
  • Approximate region-based location

Payment Data is handled entirely by app-store billing systems. We never store payment card details.

3. Use of Your Personal Data

  • Operate and improve App features.
  • Secure authentication & account access.
  • Personalized fitness recommendations.
  • Administrative notifications.
  • Analytics & reliability improvements.
  • Terms enforcement & fraud protection.

We do not sell your Personal Data.

4. Retention of Your Personal Data

Your Personal Data is retained only as long as necessary for account functionality, legal compliance, and security. When no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymized.

5. Transfer of Your Personal Data

Your data may be processed on servers outside your region. We protect your data using encryption, restricted access, and secure protocols.

6. Delete Your Personal Data

You may delete your account anytime by using the “Delete Account” option inside the App. All associated data is permanently erased. Some transaction records may temporarily remain for legal compliance.

7. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

Business Transactions: Data may transfer during mergers or acquisitions.

Law Enforcement: We may disclose data when legally required.

8. Security of Your Personal Data

  • Encryption during transfer & storage
  • Restricted internal access
  • Regular security reviews

9. Children’s Privacy

GetGood is intended for users who are 9 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 9. If we discover that information from a user younger than 9 has been submitted, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

10. Links to Other Websites

External links within the App are not under our control. Users should review third-party privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Updates will be posted within the App. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: support@getgoodapp.com
