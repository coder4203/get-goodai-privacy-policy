2. Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

2.1 Types of Data Collected

Personal Data We may collect the following information:

  • Account Information – Email address and authentication credentials for login or sign-in.
  • Profile Details – Display name, age range, gender, or fitness goals (if voluntarily provided).
  • Fitness and Performance Data – Workouts, measurements, or progress data you manually record.
  • Communication Data – Messages or feedback you send through in-app forms or email.

Usage Data Collected automatically when you interact with the App. This may include:

  • Device type, operating-system version, and unique identifiers.
  • App interaction metrics (screen views, session duration, button taps).
  • Crash logs, error diagnostics, and performance data.
  • Approximate location data (city or region) for analytic purposes.

Exercise Data When you log or track workouts, we store those entries securely under your account so that you can review progress, view analytics, and receive insights.

Payment Data Payments for subscriptions or upgrades are processed exclusively through official app-store billing systems (such as Apple App Store or Google Play Billing). We do not collect or store your credit-card or banking details. Only anonymized transaction confirmations (e.g., subscription status) are stored to verify premium access.

3. Use of Your Personal Data

We may use your Personal Data to:

  • Operate and maintain the App’s features and functionality.
  • Authenticate users and manage secure account access.
  • Provide personalized recommendations based on your fitness data and goals.
  • Send administrative communications, including security alerts, policy updates, or account-related messages.
  • Analyze user behavior and technical performance to enhance reliability and design.
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions and protect against misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

We never sell or rent your Personal Data to third parties.

4. Retention of Your Personal Data

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as needed to:

  • Maintain your account, provide requested services, and meet legal obligations.
  • Resolve disputes, troubleshoot issues, and enforce agreements.

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized. Backup copies may persist temporarily for system integrity or legal compliance.

5. Transfer of Your Personal Data

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located in other regions or countries. We take reasonable steps to ensure that transfers occur under strong protection standards, such as encryption, secure transport protocols (HTTPS/TLS), and restricted access controls. By using the App, you consent to such transfers consistent with this Policy.

6. Delete Your Personal Data

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the App at any time by tapping the “Delete Account” option in your profile or settings menu. When you confirm deletion: Your account and all stored data — including workout history, progress logs, and personal details — are permanently erased from our active systems. Certain minimal records (for example, transaction confirmations required by app-store billing systems or legal compliance) may be retained securely for a limited period before automatic removal. Once deletion is complete, your data cannot be recovered, even if you reinstall or sign up again using the same email address. If you experience any issues using the in-app deletion feature, you may also contact (notyet) for manual verification and removal.

We employ technical and organizational safeguards, including:

Despite these precautions, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security. Use the App with an understanding of these inherent limitations.
