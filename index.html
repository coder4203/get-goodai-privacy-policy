GetGood Privacy Policy Overview

Effective Date: October 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how GetGood ("the App," "We," "Us," or "Our"), developed by Yusuf Rana, collects, uses, and discloses your Personal Data when you use our fitness application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your information is handled.

1. Interpretation and Definitions

The following definitions apply to this Privacy Policy.

  • Application (App): Refers to the GetGood mobile application.
  • Company: Refers to GetGood by Yusuf Rana.
  • Account: The unique profile created for You to access our App.
  • Personal Data: Information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • Usage Data: Data automatically collected, generated by the use of the App or from the App infrastructure itself.
  • Device: Any device (e.g., phone, tablet) that can access the App.
  • Service Providers: Third-party entities employed to facilitate the App's operation and services.
  • You: The individual accessing or using the App.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We collect two main types of data: Personal Data you provide directly and Usage Data collected automatically.

2.1 Personal Data You Provide

When using our App, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including:

  • Account & Authentication: Email address, secure authentication tokens (used for login).
  • Profile Information: Display name, age range, specified fitness goals (optional).
  • Fitness & Health Data: Manually logged workouts, progress metrics, and related activity data.
  • Communication Records: Content of messages or support requests sent to us.

2.2 Automatically Collected Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the App. This may include:

  • Your Device's Internet Protocol address (IP address).
  • Device type, operating system (OS), and unique device identifiers.
  • App interaction metrics (e.g., features used, frequency of use).
  • Diagnostic data, such as crash logs and performance reports.
  • Approximate geographic location derived from IP address (region-based, not precise GPS).

2.3 Payment Information

All subscription and payment processing is handled exclusively by **third-party app-store billing systems** (e.g., Apple App Store, Google Play Store). **We do not collect, process, or store your payment card details.**

3. How Your Personal Data is Used

The Company may use Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To operate, maintain, and provide the core features of the App.
  • Account Management: To manage your Account, secure authentication, and enable access.
  • Personalization: To provide personalized fitness recommendations and track progress.
  • Communication: To send important administrative notices, updates, and support responses.
  • Analysis & Improvement: To monitor the use of the App, perform analytics, and improve reliability and features.
  • Legal Compliance: For purposes of enforcing our Terms and Conditions, preventing fraud, and meeting legal requirements.

Crucially: We do not sell your Personal Data to third parties for monetary compensation.

4. Retention of Your Personal Data

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. This includes the duration of your active Account, compliance with legal obligations (e.g., tax, accounting), and resolving disputes.

Once data is no longer necessary, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

5. Transfer of Your Personal Data

Your information, including Personal Data, may be processed and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those of your jurisdiction.

We implement safeguards to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy, utilizing measures such as **encryption**, **restricted access controls**, and **secure data protocols**.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your Personal Data.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure (Deletion): Delete your Account and associated Personal Data.

You may **delete your account at any time** using the dedicated “Delete Account” option within the App settings. All associated data will be permanently erased, subject only to a temporary retention of some transaction records for legal and financial compliance.

7. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

  • Business Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your Personal Data may be transferred. We will provide notice before your Personal Data is subject to a different Privacy Policy.
  • Law Enforcement: Under certain circumstances, we may be required to disclose your Personal Data if legally compelled to do so by government authorities or court order.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with Service Providers solely to monitor and analyze the use of our App, and for technical support.

8. Security of Your Personal Data

The security of your Personal Data is critical to us. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, including:

  • End-to-end **Encryption** for data transfer (TLS/SSL).
  • Strong encryption mechanisms for data **storage at rest**.
  • **Restricted internal access** to data based on the principle of least privilege.
  • **Regular security audits** and vulnerability assessments.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

The GetGood App is intended for users who are **9 years of age or older**. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 9.

If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from a child under 9 without verifiable parental consent, we will take immediate steps to remove that information from our servers.

10. Links to Other Websites

Our App may contain links to external websites that are not operated by Us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit, as we have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content or practices of any third-party sites or services.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Continued use of the App after the changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the new terms.
